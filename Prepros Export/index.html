Services

Getting the job of your dreams is not easy in this competitive job market. HR professionals agree that candidates who invest in marketing themselves have the most success. The most important career investment you can make is hiring a professional résumé writing service. Our professional résumé writers know how to make your résumé stand out because we are experienced job counsellors and recruiters.

Professional Resumes

A professional résumé is only as good as the information you provide. If you don't have an existing résumé we will email you a questionnaire prompting you to provide the career information needed to create your résumé.

Cover Letters

It is important to note that many employers will require a cover letter with the job application, and a well written cover letter should get you one step closer to an interview. Our customised cover letters will highlight your skills and qualifications and how these relate to the new role/company. We can tailor your cover letter for a specific job, or write a general cover letter that you can adjust to suit different positions.

Linkedin Profiles

LinkedIn is the world’s largest professional network with over 380 million members worldwide.Your profile needs to have a competitive edge over other candidates, and help you take full advantage of all the incredible features LinkedIn has to offer. We will help you develop a professional LinkedIn profile tailored to your industry, including work history and achievements.

Competency Criteria

Almost all roles look for specific skills and competencies that are relevant to the responsibilities of the role. You can enhance your prospects of success if you have a document that specifically addresses these. Based on information you provide, our writers can develop this document outlining your background - supporting it with examples from your work history. If the role you are applying for has these specific requirements then seriously consider using this additional service.

Plus

If you are a business owner who needs to engage with clients or you spend more time than you should preparing those RFI’s, sales proposals, project plans and so on then seriously consider using our services to assist you. Our professional writers can help you produce those winning documents.

RFI / RFP / RFQ / RFT RESPONSES

Anyone in business knows how time consuming and labour intensive it can be responding to requests for Information, proposal, quotation, tender. We can help you to demonstrate your ability to fulfil requirements of proposals/tenders with well-structured responses that are clear and concise with content designed for your target audience. Let us take away the majority of time consuming responses whilst you focus on running your business.

WEBSITE CONTENT

First impressions are everything in the online world. It is vital that you structure your website content to fit your target audience. We will help you to present the best first impression to web viewers with thoughtfully written content that immediately engages potential clients and encourages further exploration and contact.

SALES PROPOSALS

Impress existing clients and prospects with carefully thought out proposals for products, services and projects. We can help you produce clear, concise, and well-presented documents. Whether technical or business people, we will ensure that your proposal is written for your target audience.

PROJECT PROPOSALS

Impress and inspire project sponsors (business owners, investors) to back your project by presenting carefully written proposals. With a focus on clarity, we can help you to create clear and concise project proposals. Whether technical or business people, we will ensure that your proposal is written for your target audience

PROJECT PLANS / CHARTERS

Give clients confidence in your ability to execute and deliver successful projects. We can help you produce documents that can be understood by all parties with clear objectives, responsibilities timelines, budgets, and contingencies.
