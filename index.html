Peter

Peter has over 40 years of work experience in the IT industry. He has worked within technical and managerial roles, and in the recruitment industry as a consultant. He has successfully placed candidates in a number of industry segments, including IT, finance, accounting, and hospitality. Peter has worked with many job seekers, from recent graduates to workforce veterans, reviewing their résumés and assisting with directly targeting employer requirements. He sees that the employers’ first impressions of résumés and cover letters essentially act as a first interview with a job seeker. In the cutthroat world of recruitment, job applicants need tailored documents to engage employers and elevate themselves above the competition.
